BY-COVID (BeYond COVID)

BY-COVID (BeYond COVID) was a Horizon Europe project aiming to enhance pandemic preparedness and future infectious disease response through the development and integration of open, interoperable data resources.

SciLifeLab

SciLifeLab (Science for Life Laboratory) is funded as a national research infrastructure by the Swedish government. It brings together the unique strengths of individual researchers and infrastructure across Sweden to create state of the art life science resources.
