Pathogens Portal Nodes
Sweden

The Swedish Pathogens Portal provides secure access to genomic data, epidemiological information, and research datasets. Researchers can upload, share, and analyze pathogen data through our integrated platform with advanced analytical tools for genomic analysis and phylogenetic studies.

Norway

Norway's pathogen portal emphasizes Arctic and marine pathogen surveillance, providing specialized tools for monitoring infectious diseases in extreme environments. The platform supports research on climate change impacts on pathogen distribution and evolution.

Spain

Spain's pathogen surveillance portal focuses on Mediterranean and tropical disease monitoring, providing comprehensive tools for tracking vector-borne diseases and emerging infectious diseases in the Iberian Peninsula and surrounding regions.

Switzerland

Switzerland's pathogen portal serves as a central European hub for infectious disease surveillance, providing advanced analytical tools for outbreak detection and response coordination across the Alpine region and neighboring countries.

Netherlands

The Netherlands pathogen portal specializes in antimicrobial resistance surveillance and One Health approaches. The platform provides comprehensive tools for tracking resistant pathogens across human, animal, and environmental sectors, supporting evidence-based policy decisions.

Costa Rica

Costa Rica's pathogen portal focuses on tropical and emerging infectious diseases, providing specialized surveillance tools for monitoring vector-borne diseases, zoonotic infections, and climate-sensitive pathogens in Central America and the Caribbean region.

European Network Integration

The Swedish Pathogens Portal serves as the national node for pathogen data sharing and analysis in Sweden, integrated within the broader European network of pathogen surveillance systems. Our platform provides researchers and public health officials with comprehensive access to genomic data, epidemiological information, and analytical tools for infectious disease research and surveillance.

Through our integration with European networks, we facilitate cross-border collaboration and data sharing, enabling rapid response to emerging infectious diseases and supporting global pandemic preparedness efforts. The portal serves as a critical infrastructure for Sweden's contribution to international pathogen surveillance and research initiatives.

Data Access & Sharing

Our platform provides secure, controlled access to sensitive pathogen data while maintaining the highest standards of data protection and privacy. Researchers can access curated datasets, upload their own research data, and collaborate with international partners through our integrated sharing mechanisms.

The portal supports various data formats and provides standardized APIs for seamless integration with existing research workflows and laboratory information systems. We ensure compliance with international data sharing agreements and facilitate responsible data stewardship across the pathogen research community.

Toolbox for creating Pathogen Portal Nodes

Our comprehensive suite of analytical tools includes advanced genomic analysis pipelines, phylogenetic reconstruction algorithms, and epidemiological modeling capabilities. These tools are designed to support researchers in understanding pathogen evolution, transmission dynamics, and outbreak patterns.

The platform integrates machine learning algorithms for pathogen classification, antimicrobial resistance prediction, and outbreak risk assessment. Users can access cloud-based computational resources and collaborate on complex analyses through our interactive visualization and reporting tools.
